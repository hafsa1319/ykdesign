FLOOR COVERING INSTALLATION

Floor covering occupies a very important place in the decoration of the house. Floor covering installation is therefore a task that needs to be done carefully. We want the floor color, structure and material used to be compatible with our home and even to suit our belongings. But not everything is just that it fits into the decoration. It should provide long use with a quality material structure.

It should have a structure that does not deform easily and does not wear out quickly. Therefore, first of all you should decide on the type of flooring you are going to choose. Many flooring materials and types are available such as laminated, massive, porcelain, epoxy, marble and PVC flooring. By determining the features you are looking for, you can reach the right material and upholstery in this way.

Tile Flooring on the Floor


Tile can be produced from materials such as tile, concrete, metal and glass. Although they have uses on the floor and walls, they are mostly preferred on floors. Laying a tile floor is not a complicated process. With the supply of several necessary materials, the process is carried out quite easily.

A few materials that you will need when laying tiles on the floor, mortar to be able to glue the tiles to the floor, joint filler to fill them in, a hammer made of rubber material and a flat trowel. Before starting the laying process, it is important to know how many tiles you need and to measure the area you will make. The gaps left on the edges will not provide a pleasant and aesthetic appearance.

Ceramic Tile Flooring


Ceramic tile has become very popular recently. It is produced from ceramic material without any type of metal in it. Ceramic tile flooring has a fragile structure if they are not yet laid. Therefore, it is necessary to act carefully.

It is preferred with its affordable price. It is more affordable than other flooring materials.
With its waterproof structure, it can be easily used in bathroom and kitchen areas.
It is not easily damaged after laying.
Easy to clean. With its waterproof structure, you can clean it using soap and water.
It is available in many different options and colors. Therefore, you can easily find the one that adapts to your home decoration.
How to Lay Tile on Plywood?


It is produced from plywood wood species and is often preferred from the flooring industry. You can usually choose other materials such as tiles, tiles and plywood as a subfloor. In particular, it allows uneven floors to stand up straighter and make flooring easier. But if you want to lay tiles on plywood, you must first choose a thin material and not raise the floor too much. The drying time for this procedure, which is carried out with a special glue, is from 3 to 4 days. Another point to note is that the adhesive is applied on the tile, not on the plywood. Tile flooring on a flat and solid floor increases the strength of the flooring.

Tile Flooring Prices


Taking into account the square meter and workmanship in which the flooring is made, different price ranges will emerge for each of your rooms. The material to be chosen, the subfloor covering and the prices differ from each other. It will be more affordable, especially if ceramic tile is chosen. You can get information for the most affordable flooring among many different alternatives such as tile flooring on concrete or ceramic tile flooring.
