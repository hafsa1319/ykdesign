FAQ

What is LVT?

It stands for LVT (LUXURY VINLY TILE). It has become a preferred product in almost every place in recent years because it has a wear-resistant, hygienic, flexible and comfortable nature.

Why is LVT Preferred?

It is often preferred because it is antibacterial and it has a transparent pattern, variety of different options, wear-protective layer, sound-absorbing filling layer and size stabilizer.

What is the Difference of LVT Flooring from Other Floor Coverings?

LVT Floorings have a softer surface than other flooring materials such as wood, marble, stone. They provide better quality performance and are preferred with comfortable foot circulation, sound reduction, and aesthetic appearance.

What is the Cost Compared to Other Floor Coverings?

Traditional parquet, wood, laminate, and stone flooring options are less costly than LVT flooring. It is an alternative product for those who want a durable flooring at a much lower cost.

Where is it Used?

It can be applied at Hospital, Home, Office, School, Hotel, Gymnasium, Cinema Halls, Meeting Halls, Nursery, Kindergarten, Children's Playgrounds, Universities, Conference Halls and all interior spaces.
